About

Simple, honest landscaping support.

Carrick Landscaping is run by William Carrick and focuses on helping customers improve, tidy and maintain their outdoor areas. The service is ideal for homeowners looking for reliable help with garden care, basic landscaping and seasonal outdoor maintenance.

Whether you need a one-off tidy-up or regular support, William can discuss the work needed and provide a clear quote.

Important

Material and rubble removal is not included.

Carrick Landscaping does not remove material, rubble, soil, garden waste, branches, grass cuttings or other waste from site. Customers should arrange disposal separately, such as through their local council, skip hire or another waste removal service.

This keeps the service focused on landscaping and garden work, while making sure waste disposal is handled safely and correctly.
